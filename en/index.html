ABOUT US

  • “Azeri Mining Group” LLC has been operating in mining industry since 2018.
  • The company carries out overburden, drilling, blasting, rock transportation and recultivation works.
  • In 2019, AZERI MINING GROUP LLC conducted drilling operations at the gold mine in Gadabay region, the Chovdar gold mine in Dashkesan region, the porphyry deposit belonging to Gizilja Girmadash plant in Goygol region and the limestone quarry in Gazakh region.
  • The company performed overburden, drilling and loading works to extract kaolinite from the Chanlibel kaolinite deposit located in Shamkir region from 2019 to 2020. Works on that mining field have been underway since May 2021
  • We are the member of the German-Azerbaijani Chamber of Commerce (AHK Azerbaijan) since 2021.
