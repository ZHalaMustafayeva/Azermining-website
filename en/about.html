ABOUT US

QUALITY

“Azeri Mining Group” LLC carries out its policy in workplaces by setting following principles mentioned below:

  • To affirm the reputation as a reliable company that prioritizes its obligations to its customers, partners and employees
  • Strictly comply with all applicable laws, regulations and other requirements of the Quality Management System
  • Providing possession and implementation of the latest technology for keeping the company in high level and competitive
  • Take measures to continuously improve the Quality Management System (QMS) as well as to further improve performance
  • Improving the profiessionality of the employees at the company and involving the whole team into ensuring the quality of the work
  • Continuously develop and improve KIS by conducting internal audits to ensure compliance with the requirements of ISO 9001: 2015
Services

The management of Azeri Mining Group LLC undertakes:

  • Bringing the policy of the company on Occupational safety and environmental protection to the attention of all employees
  • Providing the necessary resources for the implementation of above-mentioned principles
  • To help all employees of the company to achieve the set goal

  • “Azeri Mining Group” LLC has been operating in mining industry since 2018. The company carries out overburden, drilling, blasting, rock transportation and recultivation works.

    2018

  • 2019

    AZERI MINING GROUP LLC conducted drilling operations at the gold mine in Gadabay region, the Chovdar gold mine in Dashkesan region, the porphyry deposit belonging to Gizilja Girmadash plant in Goygol region and the limestone quarry in Gazakh region.

  • The company performed overburden, drilling and loading works to extract kaolinite from the Chanlibel kaolinite deposit located in Shamkir region from 2019 to 2020. Works on that mining field have been underway since May 2021. Apart from that, the company fulfilled peacekeeping drilling and blasting works in Firuzabad village of Goygol region, Gizilja Girmadash plant and Laki station of Agdash region.

  • 2021

    Starting from November 2020, we have been performing drilling, blasting and transportation works at the Agyokhush gold mine of AzerGold CJSC. From July 2021, these works will also be carried out at the Marah gold mine in Dashkesan region.
