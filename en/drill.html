We are executing works on topographic measurements for drilling wells together with the customer company, drilling wells with special drilling equipment (Atlas Copco, Furukawa) after flagging and numbering of the wells by \ the customer company indicating the depth and diameter of the wells, taking samples from drilled wells for laboratory analysis

Filling of pre-drilled wells with explosives in accordance with the results of laboratory analysis, evacuation and placement of equipment and personnel in a safe area, re-inspection and safety measures by security and HSE staff, site blasting, inspection of the area for inspection , we carry out stripping work in accordance with the results of laboratory tests, return of evacuated equipment and manpower to the mining area and start the transportation work.
